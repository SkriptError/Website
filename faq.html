Frequently Asked Questions




What is Aero Client?

Aero Client is a reskin of CheatBreaker, with way more features added!

Is it safe & free?

Yes, Aero Client is safe and free to download.

How do I get cosmetics on the client?

You get cosmetics by applying for a media rank in our Discord.

How can I request for something to be added to Aero Client?

You may suggest new features in our Discord.

Can I use my own mods?

No, you may only use mods included in Aero Client.

Does Aero Client boost frames?

Aero Client will often more than double your frames in game.
